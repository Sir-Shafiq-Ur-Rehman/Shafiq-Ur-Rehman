A Lifelong Pursuit of Knowledge

As a seasoned educator with a Master of Science in Applied Mathematics, his career has been a testament to a deep-seated passion for the sciences. He specializes in making complex concepts in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry accessible and engaging for O-Level and A-Level students. His pedagogical approach is built on fostering intellectual curiosity and revealing the elegant interplay between scientific theory and real-world application.

Throughout his extensive teaching history, he has had the privilege of heading mathematics departments, developing curricula, and mentoring hundreds of students toward academic excellence. He is now seeking a challenging new role within a forward-thinking academic institution and also welcomes inquiries for individual student mentoring, where he can continue to inspire the next generation of thinkers.
